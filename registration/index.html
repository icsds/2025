Registration

  • Payment of the registration fee is mandatory for all participants.
  • All fees are listed in Canadian dollars (CAD).
  • Please note that you can pay the registration fee via PayPal using a credit or debit card. Simply click the “Checkout” button, and you will be directed to the Order Summary page where You can pay with a debit or credit card.
  • A full refund, excluding PayPal service fees, will be issued for cancellations requested on or before May 1, 2025. No refunds will be provided for requests submitted after this date.
Category Registration Fee
Faculty/Industry $480
Students/Postdocs $280

  • A receipt will be sent to you via email. If you require a formal receipt, please contact Jiguo Cao at jiguo_cao@sfu.ca.
