Hotels

A list of hotels near the conference venue can be found here.

The Harbour Centre is located at the Waterfront SkyTrain station, which is the terminal stop of the Canada Line and Expo Line. The SkyTrain map can be found here. One may also opt to book a hotel in any area along the Canada Line or Expo Line or close to other skytrain stations; for instance, hotels near Yaletown-Roundhouse station (5-minutes ride to Waterfront) can be found here. Hotels near Richmond Aberdeen Center (25-minute ride to Waterfront) can be found here.

Another option is to book accommodations through airbnb.ca . Vancouver is a very safe city, and public transit—especially the SkyTrain and buses—is reliable and runs frequently. You can find great Airbnb options throughout the Metro Vancouver area, including cities like Burnaby, Richmond, and Coquitlam. As long as your accommodation is near a SkyTrain station, you should be able to reach the conference venue within approximately 30 minutes. We recommend using Google Maps to check travel times and plan your route to the conference.
